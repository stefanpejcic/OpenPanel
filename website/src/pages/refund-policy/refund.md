## Refund Eligibility

A refund may be requested only if ALL of the following conditions are met:

- **The license has NOT been activated**. Once a license key is activated on any server or environment, the license is considered used and no refund will be issued under any circumstances.
- The refund request is submitted within 7 calendar days of the original purchase date.
- The request is made by the original purchasing account holder through our support system.

> **Refunds will NOT be issued for:**
> - Any activated license, regardless of usage duration.
> - Requests submitted more than 7 days after purchase.
> - Dissatisfaction with features that are publicly documented (including the Docker-per-user architecture).
> - Partial billing periods on monthly or annual subscriptions.
> - Subscription renewals (monthly or annual).

## How to Request a Refund

To submit a refund request, contact our support team via [this support ticket](https://my.openpanel.com/submitticket.php?step=2&deptid=1) or at [info@openpanel.com](mailto:info@openpanel.com) within 7 days of purchase. Your request must include:
- Your order number and registered email address.
- Confirmation that the license has not been activated.
- A brief reason for the request.

We reserve the right to verify activation status before approving any refund. Approved refunds will be returned to the original payment method (PayPal, or Stripe) within 5–10 business days.
