# How do I cancel my license in the my.openpanel.com?

To cancel a license purchased through the my.openpanel.com website, follow the steps below:

1. Log in to your [my.openpanel.com](https://my.openpanel.com/index.php?rp=/login) account.
   ![openpanel login form](img/whmcs/1_login.png)
2. Navigate to **Licenses**, then click **My Licenses**.
   ![list licenses](img/whmcs/2_license.png)
3. Click on **Active** next to the license you wish to cancel.
   ![select license](img/whmcs/3_active.png)
4. Click **Cancel**.
   ![cancel](img/whmcs/4_cancel.png)
5. Click on **Request Cancellation**.
   ![confirm](img/whmcs/5_request_cancel.png)

- If *Immediate* is selected: your license will be immediately terminated.
- If *End of billing period* is selected: your license will be terminated at the end of the billing period and tou will no longer receive invoices for it.
