# OpenPanel Refund Policy

New OpenPanel Enterprise licenses purchased through my.openpanel.com are eligible for a refund within **7 days** of the purchase date, **provided the license has not been activated**. Once a license key is activated, no refund will be issued regardless of the time elapsed since purchase.

> **Note**: This policy only applies to **new license purchases** and does not include license renewals.

[Refund Policy](/refund-policy)

## Requesting a Refund

To request a refund for an eligible new license, please [submit a request](https://my.openpanel.com/submitticket.php?step=2&deptid=1) to our Customer Service team.

## Renewals and Cancellations

**Important**: If your license auto-renewed or is canceled after the 72-hour refund window (Only applies to new license purchases), it will not qualify for a refund.

In such cases, you can follow the steps to cancel the license using this article: [How do I cancel my OpenPanel license?](/docs/articles/license/hot_to_cancel_openpanel_license)
