# Passkeys

Passkeys are a modern, phishing-resistant alternative to passwords. Instead of typing a password, you authenticate using your device — via biometrics (fingerprint or face), a PIN, or a hardware security key.

## Enable Passkeys

To add a passkey to your account, click the 'Add Passkey' button.

Your browser will prompt you to authenticate using your device's built-in method — such as Touch ID, Face ID, Windows Hello, or a hardware security key. Once confirmed, the passkey is registered to your account.

## Using a Passkey to Log In
On the login page, click 'Sign in with a Passkey'. Your browser will ask you to verify your identity using the same device method you used during registration. No password is required.

## Managing Passkeys
You can register multiple passkeys: for example, one on your laptop and one on your phone. Each registered passkey is listed in your account settings, where you can remove any that are no longer needed.
